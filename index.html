Story

In a small poor town surrended by a big forest, a little girl lived with her family, everyone was afraid of the forest and told storys that there lived creatures that eated human flesh, but her father was not afraid and explored it at any given chance, untill a day he comes home sick and the only way to save him was to get a cure from the creatures... So this little girl adventure beggins and she finds herselfe having to make many choices that may or may not change everything she used to know.

A game full of mystery, adventure and fantasy, what end will you choose, who will you save?
